How This Protects You

No name. No phone number. No email. Ever.

01

You get a passphrase

Shown once, on your screen, never stored by us. It is derived into a keypair locally, in your browser. We only ever see the public half.

02

Your report and media are encrypted to your pseudonymous key before they ever touch a server. Files are stripped of EXIF and location metadata first.

03

Re-enter your passphrase later to unlock your mailbox. No "online now" status, no read receipts — nothing that could place you anywhere.
