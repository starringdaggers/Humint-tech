How This Protects You

No name. No phone number. No email. No account. Ever.

01

You create a passphrase

Generated on your device. Shown to you exactly once. The moment you confirm you've saved it, we delete our only copy — from then on, only you have it.

02

Your passphrase is your identity

There's no username, no password reset, no "forgot passphrase" link. The same way a Web3 wallet works — whoever holds the phrase holds the access. We can't recover it, and we can't be made to hand it over, because we never have it.

03

You come back anytime

Re-enter your passphrase later to view replies. No login history, no "last seen," nothing that places you anywhere in time or space.
